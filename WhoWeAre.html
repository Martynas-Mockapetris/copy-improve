EUROTECH IS SPORTS AND ENTERTAINMENT EXPERIENCE

Eurotech Global Sports AG is a Swiss-based event/sports IT services company. Our staff has event experience in fifteen countries in the past eleven yers and is dedicated to providing high value-added planning, project management and operations support for the implementation and deployment of IT services to large-scale events. Most recently a major focus is to leverage the technical and event expertise of the Eurotech team for the development and support of Event Management software. This initiative is supported by a truly global team with team members around the world and concentrations in the United States, Europe and South America.

OUR TEAM

The Eurotech team consists of over 1000 combined years of event and entertainment experience. Our team members have worked on major and minor sporting events including:
  • 6 FIFA World Cups
  • 5 Olympic Games
  • Athletics, Figure Skating and Cycling World Championships
  • Various national and club championships
