Swiss information Technology Company Eurotech Global Sports AG (EGS) is in the business of organization and operational management of IT-Systems and services especially for major sports and entertainment events. EGS provides a wide range of information technology and consulting services to support any type of event, large or small. If you need us to assist you in defining a global IT Infrastructure for your event or you require a specific service such as an accreditation or volunteer management solution for your event, we can deliver. Eurotech is able to provide you with the right solution, at the right time. We specialize in:

  • Technology Solution management
  • Program and Project management
  • Architecture and Systems Integration
  • Networking and Telecommunications
  • Event Operations
  • Event Applications
  • Ticketing and Access Control Solutions

A Proven Track Record of Success

Eurotech Global Sports AG has been successfully involved in the delivery of the IT Solution for the 2014 FIFA World Cup Brazil™, the 2010 FIFA World Cup South Africa™, the 2006 FIFA World Cup Germany™ and the 2002 FIFA World Cup Korea / Japan™. At each fo these events, FIFA contracted EGS as the global systems integrator responsible for overseeing the delivery of the IT Solution for these events.

The EGS team was responsible for many key areas including the definition of the overall systems and network architecture. They created the integration and operations plans that were supported by the full IT Solution team. They coordinated all IT Related activities for sponsors, partners, vendors and suppliers. EGS staff led all integration, deployment and operations activities as they related to the successful delivery of the IT Solution.

This all culminated in the successful delivery of IT services to one of the most complex sporting events ever, and successful delivery was achieved under unique and challenging circumstances.

In addition to the FIFA World Cup™ events, the EGS team has fulfilled consulting and operational and IT delivery roles in other large and small scale sporting events throughout the world, including IAAF Athletics World Championships, Olympic Games, US Figure Skating, UCI cycling events and various bid committees.
